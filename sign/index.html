Moving the Millions

New York Transit Museum
Boerum Pl & Schermerhorn St









Exhibit




Moving the Millions highlights the evolution of the subway and the major issues and events that influenced the development of the largest transportation network in North America. to twenty vintage subway and elevated cars dating back to 1907, the Museums working platform level spans a full city block.
